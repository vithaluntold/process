# EPI X-Ray - Competitive Analysis & Feature Comparison (2025)

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive feature-by-feature comparison of EPI X-Ray against the top 10 process mining platforms in the market. Each platform is scored across 15 key dimensions on a scale of 1-10, with detailed analysis of capabilities.

**Overall Market Position: EPI X-Ray ranks #4 overall with strong advantages in accessibility, modern technology, and AI integration.**

## Scoring Methodology

Each platform is evaluated across 15 categories:
1. **Process Discovery** - Ability to automatically discover process models from event logs
2. **Conformance Checking** - Validation of actual processes against defined models
3. **Performance Analytics** - KPI tracking, bottleneck identification, cycle time analysis
4. **AI/ML Capabilities** - Predictive analytics, anomaly detection, intelligent recommendations
5. **Automation Integration** - RPA connectivity and automation opportunity identification
6. **Data Connectivity** - Pre-built connectors, data source flexibility
7. **Real-Time Analysis** - Live process monitoring and streaming analytics
8. **Visualization** - Process flow diagrams, dashboards, reporting quality
9. **Ease of Use** - Learning curve, user interface, onboarding simplicity
10. **Implementation Speed** - Time from purchase to first insights
11. **Scalability** - Ability to handle enterprise-scale data volumes
12. **Pricing/Value** - Cost relative to features and ROI
13. **Security & Compliance** - GDPR, SOC2, data protection, audit logging
14. **Task Mining** - Desktop-level user activity capture
15. **Simulation** - What-if scenario analysis and digital twin capabilities

**Scoring Scale:**
- **9-10:** Industry-leading capability
- **7-8:** Strong capability, competitive advantage
- **5-6:** Good capability, meets requirements
- **3-4:** Basic capability, needs improvement
- **1-2:** Minimal or no capability
